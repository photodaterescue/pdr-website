Refund Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2025

We aim to deliver software that is premium, reliable, and genuinely helpful. Because PDR provides a free web demo and publishes clear system requirements, refunds are limited and assessed carefully.

1. Refund Window

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of purchase.

2. Valid Reasons for Refund

A refund may be approved if:

  • the software fails to function as advertised,
  • a technical issue cannot be resolved after reasonable support attempts,
  • the activation system fails permanently and cannot be restored.

3. Not Eligible for Refund

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • user error or incorrect usage,
  • unsupported or corrupted file types,
  • loss of data resulting from lack of backups,
  • dissatisfaction with results after full processing (the demo exists for testing),
  • misuse of licences or attempts to bypass lifetime-eligibility rules.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email: admin@photodaterescue.com

Please copy/paste and complete the template below:

Refund Request Template (required)

Subject: Refund Request – PDR Purchase Name: Email used at checkout: Order number: Date of purchase: Operating system (Windows/Mac): Description of the issue: (Please explain what happened and what you tried.) Have you contacted support previously? Yes/No If yes, include the support ticket reference or date.

Refunds, if approved, are processed securely by Lemon Squeezy, our payment provider.
