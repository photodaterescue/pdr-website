Tools & Recommendations

Our curated list of utilities and hardware for the ultimate photo workflow.

Essential Toolkit

  • Storage: We recommend SSDs for your working library (speed matters when processing thousands of files) and HDDs for cold storage backups.
  • Deduplication: While PDR handles date fixing, dedicated deduplication tools can help clear out exact copies before you start.
  • Viewers: Fast image viewers like IrfanView (Windows) or XnView can help you quickly browse large collections.
  • Backup Strategy: Follow the 3-2-1 rule: 3 copies of data, 2 different media types, 1 offsite. Once PDR fixes your timeline, back it up immediately!
