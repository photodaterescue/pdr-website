Social Apps

Recovering timelines from chat histories and social media downloads.

Handling Social Media Exports

  • WhatsApp: WhatsApp strips EXIF metadata but often encodes the date in the filename (e.g., IMG-20230101-WA0001). PDR parses these filenames to restore the correct date taken.
  • Messenger / Telegram / Signal: Similar to WhatsApp, these apps often modify metadata. PDR uses file attributes and naming patterns to reconstruct the timeline.
  • Snapchat / TikTok: Downloaded clips often lose their original creation time. PDR can help place them back in context based on export dates or filenames.
  • Consolidating Chats: If you have multiple backups from different years or devices, put them all in one folder. PDR will sort them out chronologically.
