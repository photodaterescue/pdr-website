Digitising Old Memories

  • Scanning Workflow: When scanning, try to organise files into folders named by year or event (e.g., "1995 - Summer Vacation"). PDR can infer dates from folder names.
  • Metadata Injection: Scanned files usually have the "scan date" as the creation date. PDR allows you to batch-correct these to the estimated "content date" if you have folder cues.
  • Mixed Formats: Don't worry if your scans are BMP, TIFF, or JPEG. PDR handles various formats and helps standardise them for modern libraries.
  • Pre-Digital Era: Use PDR to shift timestamps of scanned collections to their approximate decades, ensuring they appear before your digital photos in timeline views.
